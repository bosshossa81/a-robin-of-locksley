To Live Through Stories

My whole life, I've been drawn to stories whether they be movies, shows, books, comics or video games. I've spent so much of my life enveloped in these stories that my mind can only think in them now and I've inherently became a storyteller. So, below is a collection of these stories and poems that I've developed over the years as I've practiced my writing skills. Some of them are fun and light reminiscenences, some are dark and reflective contemplations, and some are just nonsensical goofs. A fitting representation of the one I call me.

Legend:

  • @ represents works with reflections or commentary from me
  • ₡ represents now refined works whose origins trace back to my undergraduate studies
  • ℝ represents works with some potentially mature content, such as vulgar language, drug use, violence, etc.
  • Not Published represents works that are currently being edited or formatted for publication on the page
