Walkin' the Tracks

Inspired by the film "Trainspotting"

Wha’s a wee prick
te be free of yer body.
The world round losses i’s weigh
ye canne een feel a nick.

Wha’s a few hundred quid
for a momen o’ eternal bliss?
The world round frozen in time
wit no worries abou wha ye did.

Wha’s a future as a worker
when the presen can las
a lifetime. If I wanne a’live their life,
I’da move te Yoker.

I loss six years of me life,
most o’ it on skag.
Ol’ Ronnie ha me bea by two.
Lucky bastard, wen ou wit a knife.

I mighe be jus a junkie,
but can ye blame me.
T’allows me te be free,
free o’ the weighe o’ possibility.

“Mark, ye jus need te choose life”
Tha’s wha me bird use’a say.
Course, she flew the coup
after all the strife.

I’m mostly te blame.
Who would wanne stay
with a ween as yer companion?
Who would wanne live wit the shame?

Me life’s slowly fadin away.
I’ve no job, no prospects,
jus the damn allure o’ me addiction.
Me own life I betray.

I need te get clean.
If fuckin Obi Wan Kenobi can
why canne I.
I need te break routine.

From here on ou, I live as an aro,
from heroin tha’s. No more needles
jus the life I’m choosin te live.
